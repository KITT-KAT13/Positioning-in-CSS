The Warrior Scroll

At a small-town museum, an author named Mr. Lorne and his daughter Ella were exploring ancient displays. Behind an ornamental vase, Ella spotted an old scroll in a section marked “No admittance.”

Dad, that scroll isn’t listed on the display board,” she whispered.

Mr. Lorne, intrigued, took a closer look. It was a forgotten manuscript—a rare scroll that had been misplaced years ago due to a filing error. Suddenly, alarms buzzed, lights flashed, and guards came rushing in.

There was uproar in the museum. Staff thought someone had tried to steal the scroll, and everyone was sent into quarantine while the situation was sorted out.

But after the confusion, the museum thanked Ella for her discovery.
